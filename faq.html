On the day

What time should I arrive?
We would never let you celebrate alone! Plus-ones will be mentioned on the invites. Please add their names on the RSVPs so we know who will be joining you. If we've accidentally missed a loved one off the list it certainly wasn't our intention, so if you're not sure, just ask us.
The church and venue are around half an hour's drive apart. We're looking into providing a shuttle bus from the church to the venue and back to your accommodation, so you can celebrate to the fullest. This isn't fully coordinated yet, so please let us know on your RSVP if you'd like this as an option — we appreciate that not everyone will have a car available. Taxis to the church from your accommodation of choice are very affordable and easy to arrange.
