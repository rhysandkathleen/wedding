Getting to Aitoliko

Travel & flights

Aitoliko sits on the west coast of mainland Greece. Preveza is your closest, easiest airport — but if you fancy island-hopping before or after the wedding, the Ionian islands are right on the doorstep. Here's the lay of the land.

The easy option

PVKClosest · ~1 hr 15 min drive

Preveza / Aktion (Άκτιο)

By far the most convenient. A scenic 1¼-hour drive south along the coast brings you straight to Aitoliko. Seasonal summer flights run from the UK and several European cities — book early, as they're limited. Hire a car at the airport, or we can help arrange a transfer.

🚗 ~75 km · 1 hr 15 min ✈ Seasonal summer flights

The reliable option

ATH~3 hr 30 min drive

Athens International

The fallback with the most flights, year-round, from almost everywhere. It's a straightforward (if longer) 3½-hour motorway drive west across the country — the road crosses the spectacular Rio–Antirrio bridge. Great if you want a couple of days in Athens first.

🚗 ~260 km · 3 hr 30 min ✈ Year-round, many flights

Make a holiday of it

The Ionian islands

If you're tagging a beach holiday onto the trip, fly into one of these and reach the mainland by ferry. They each have their own airport with summer flights from across Europe.

PVK

Lefkada (Λευκάδα)

The only Ionian island you can reach by car — a causeway links it straight to the mainland, no ferry needed. Fly into Preveza (PVK) just up the coast, then drive across. Famous for dramatic west-coast beaches like Porto Katsiki.

🚗 Drive across · no ferry~30 min from Preveza
EFL

Kefalonia (Κεφαλονιά)

Turquoise coves and the famous Myrtos beach. Ferries run to Kyllini on the mainland; from there it's a pretty drive north to Aitoliko.

⛴ Ferry + 🚗 drive~4 hr total
ZTH

Zakynthos / Zante (Ζάκυνθος)

Home of Shipwreck Beach and loggerhead turtles. The Kyllini ferry connects you to the mainland and the road north to us.

⛴ Ferry + 🚗 drive~3 hr 30 min
CFU

Corfu (Κέρκυρα)

The greenest, most cosmopolitan Ionian island. Reach the mainland via the Igoumenitsa ferry, then drive down. Best for a longer, lush escape.

⛴ Ferry + 🚗 drive~4 hr total
