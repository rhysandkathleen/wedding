Thursday & Saturday · 10 & 12 June 2027

How the days unfold

A laid-back beach day on the Thursday, then a Greek ceremony, long tables and dancing into the early hours on the Saturday. Here's the order of it all so you know where to be — and when.

Thursday

10 June 2027

Optional & very informal

We'd love a very informal drink and chill with anyone who fancies it the day before. Probably down at the beach, trying to squeeze in one last bit of relaxation before the big day begins! We've no idea what it'll look like yet, but we'll know nearer the time.

Saturday

12 June 2027 · The wedding day
  1. 6–6:15 PM

    Arrive & find shade

    Arrival at the church

    Please start arriving around now. There are trees for some welcome shade and the views around are lovely — a perfect chance to get a few group photos in with your close ones.

    Directions →
  2. 6:30 PM

    A little different

    Arrival of the bride

    Time to welcome the bride! A bit different for those from England — everyone follows the bride and groom into the church this time. The church is quite small and most seats are reserved for close family and friends, but do grab one if you see it. Feel free to come in and out as you please, and get some fresh air if needed.

  3. 6:30–7:45 PM

    The Ceremony

    Panagia Finikia

    Παναγία Φοινικιά · Εισόδια της Θεοτόκου

    A traditional Greek Orthodox ceremony in a traditional Greek church. For anyone not quite sure what's going on (you'll probably be in the same boat as Rhys!), our programme will hopefully help you follow along and understand a bit more.

  4. 8:15–9 PM

    In between

    Travel to the venue

    While we get some photos taken — with some of you, too — please make your way to the venue, where there'll be welcome drinks and nibbles on your arrival.

    Shuttle bus?

    Keep an eye on updates here to see if we've managed to organise a shuttle bus to and from the venue.

    Directions →
  5. 9:30–9:45 PM

    The evening begins

    We arrive!

    Sorry to keep you waiting — time to start the evening! While we wait for things to be finalised for dinner, we'll cut the cake and have a few drinks.

  6. 10–10:30 PM

    Find your seat

    Seating & speeches

    Everyone to find their seats and listen to some hopefully well-rehearsed speeches…

  7. 10:30–11:30 PM

    Dinner is served

    Food!!

    Finally, the food is served! Enjoy some Greek cuisine and a few of our favourite dishes.

  8. 11:45 PM

    To the dancefloor

    First dance

    Get up on the dancefloor with us and start dancing the night — and early morning — away! Time to grab some drinks and enjoy the rest of the evening.

  9. 2:30–5 AM

    Till the early hours

    Departures

    The venue will be closing at 5am at the latest, so dance with us while you can! If there is a shuttle bus, departure times will be confirmed nearer the time.

Dress code

Go for it!

We will be wearing our best, so we are more than happy for you to join us! Weddings do not happen often, so ladies get your heels and fascinators out and gentlemen you know what to do.

The weather

Warm & golden

Mid-June in western Greece is glorious — around 28°C by day, balmy evenings. Bring sunscreen and a light layer for night.
